O son of Neleus! awful Nestor, tell How he, the mighty Agamemnon, fell; By what strange fraud Aegysthus wrought, relate (By force he could not) such a hero's fate? Live Menelaus not in Greece? or where Was then the martial brother's pious care? Condemn'd perhaps some foreign short to tread; Or sure Aegysthus had not dared the deed."
