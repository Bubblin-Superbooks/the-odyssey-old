This Hermes gave, then, gliding off the glade, Shot to Olympus from the woodland shade. While, full of thought, revolving fates to come, I speed my passage to the enchanted dome. Arrived, before the lofty gates I stay'd; The lofty gates the goddess wide display'd; She leads before, and to the feast invites; I follow sadly to the magic rites. Radiant with starry studs, a silver seat Received my limbs: a footstool eased my feet, She mix'd the potion, fraudulent of soul; The poison mantled in the golden bowl.
