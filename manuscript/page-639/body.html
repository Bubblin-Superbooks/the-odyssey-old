'Hush thee (he cried, soft whispering in my ear), Speak not a word, lest any Greek may hear'-- And then (supporting on his arm his head), 'Hear me, companions! (thus aloud he said:) Methinks too distant from the fleet we lie: E'en now a vision stood before my eye, And sure the warning vision was from high: Let from among us some swift courier rise, Haste to the general, and demand supplies.'

"Up started Thoas straight, Andraemon's son, Nimbly he rose, and cast his garment down! Instant, the racer vanish'd off the ground; That instant in his cloak I wrapp'd me round: And safe I slept, till brightly-dawning shone The morn conspicuous on her golden throne.
