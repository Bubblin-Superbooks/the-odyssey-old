Thus speaks the queen, still dubious, with disguise Touch'd at her words, the king with warmth replies "Alas for this! what mortal strength can move The enormous burden, who but Heaven above? It mocks the weak attempts of human hands! But the whole earth must move if Heaven commands Then hear sure evidence, while we display Words seal'd with sacred truth and truth obey: This hand the wonder framed; an olive spread Full in the court its ever verdant head.
