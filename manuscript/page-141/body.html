Threat on, till all thy stores in waste decay. True, Greece affords a train of lovely dames, In wealth and beauty worthy of our flames: But never from this nobler suit we cease; For wealth and beauty less than virtue please."

To whom the youth: "Since then in vain I tell My numerous woes, in silence let them dwell.
